Delicious Bread Toast!

Description

Bread Toast is one of easiest food to make in the world. It's simple But also delicious. We are gonna make a special bread toast. So let's make it!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Add some butter to a fry pan and wait for it to melt.
  2. When it's melted, add bread to it.
  3. Heat the two parts of the bread in the butter.
  4. Once the bread become brown color, add sugar two both side of it.
  5. Then add milk to the bread.
  6. Serve it in a plate.
  7. Bread Milk Toast ready!
