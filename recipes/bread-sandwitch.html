Crispy Bread Sandwitch

Description

The bread sandwitch is really easy to make. But it's really tasty also. So let's make it!

Ingredients

  • Cooked small boneless peices of chicken
  • Two slices Bread
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. heat the bread slices until it become browm.
  2. spread the ketchup and mayonnaise in the slices.
  3. Add The chicken pieces.
  4. Bread sandwitch ready!
