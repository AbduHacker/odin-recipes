Let's make Payasam

Description

Payasam is one of the most delicious desert. We are gonna make the palada payasam. So let's get started!

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Ada (Rice flakes)

Steps

  1. Rinse half cup ada in water first.
  2. Boil 2 cups of water. Then remove from flame.
  3. Add rinsed ada to it and set aside for 15 minutes.
  4. Transfer to a strainer. Rinse it with Water to avoid further cooking.
  5. Add 1 litre of milk to a pot.
  6. Let it come to a boil. Mix it well and let it simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Add half cup of sugar.
  8. Mix it well.
  9. Let it boil in low medium flame.
  10. Let it reduce to almost half. It will take at least 45 minutes.
  11. Add ada.
  12. Mix it well.
  13. Simmer until it become thick.
  14. And paysam ready!
